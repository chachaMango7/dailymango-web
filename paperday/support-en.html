Frequently Asked Questions

I'm not receiving daily notifications

Go to your device Settings → Notifications → Paperday and make sure notifications are enabled. You can also adjust the notification time within the app's settings screen.

The quote isn't updating

Make sure you have an internet connection and restart the app. The latest quotes will sync automatically. You can also read over 1,000 quotes offline.

My saved favorites disappeared

Favorites are stored locally on your device. If you delete and reinstall the app, your favorites may be lost. Please be careful when removing the app.

Can I remove ads?

Currently, only the free version is available. We're considering an ad-free option for the future.

I'd like to suggest a quote

We always welcome great quote recommendations. Please send them to the email above.

Are there plans for other languages?

We currently support Korean and English. Additional languages such as Japanese are under consideration.
