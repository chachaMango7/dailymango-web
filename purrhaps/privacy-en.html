Privacy Policy

Effective date: To be set at launch

Rolling Mango Corp. (the "Company") protects your privacy under a principle of minimal collection. This policy explains what the App "Purrhaps" collects and how it is processed.

1. Information We Collect

  • Nickname, birthday, and cat's name: entered by you and stored only on your device (local storage). Your birthday is used to compute a personalized saju-based fortune on your device and is not sent to our servers.
  • App settings (e.g., notification on/off, reminder time): stored only on your device.
  • Anonymous authentication identifier: an anonymous session token for server features, not linked to identifying information such as name or email.

2. How We Use Information

  • Computing a personalized fortune based on your birthday (on-device)
  • Delivering fortune reminders
  • Server-side verification to prevent abuse of in-app purchases and ad rewards

3. Retention and Deletion

Your nickname, birthday, cat's name, and app settings are stored on your device and removed when you delete the App. The anonymous session is retained only as needed to provide server features.

4. Processors and Third Parties

  • Supabase: server infrastructure used for anonymous authentication, serving fortune data, in-app purchase receipt verification, and ad-reward server verification.
  • Apple App Store · Google Play: handle in-app purchase processing and receipt issuance for paid Churu packs. Payment method details (such as card numbers) are handled by each store; the Company does not collect or store them.
  • Google AdMob: may process advertising identifiers under its own policies to serve rewarded ads.

5. Advertising Identifiers

The App offers rewarded ads via Google AdMob, and an advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID) may be used in the process.

  • iOS: You can disable ad tracking under Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
  • Android: You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads under Settings → Google → Ads.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not primarily directed to children under 14, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.

7. Your Rights

You can edit your nickname, birthday, and cat's name directly in the App, or delete all local data by removing the App.

8. Contact

For privacy inquiries: info@rollingmango.co.kr

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, we will notify you via an app update or this page.
